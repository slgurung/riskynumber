RiskyNumber:

RiskyNumber is a web platform for financial information analytics, primarily for the stock market data. This is not a commercial product yet. At the moment, this platform is in it's very early stages of development.

The stock price data and majority of other information are obtained from the third party APIs and various websites using APIs and web-scraping technologies based on Python. The accuracy of the financial data and other information you find in this platform can't be gauranteed and do not constitute an investment advice to buy or sell any stocks mentioned in this website. Please read disclaimer page.

I would like to thank to all the providers of open source technologies, free APIs, tutorials, and all other tools which made this project possible. I hope this app becomes a useful source for the investors as it matures in future. Please visit the platform more frequently for the updates. Thank you for the visit.

Surya Gurung.
