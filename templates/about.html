RiskyNumber:

RiskyNumber is a web platform for financial information analytics, primarily for the stock market data. This is not a commercial product. This platform is being developed for personal education & research purposes only. At the moment, this platform is in it's very early stages of development.

The majority of information is derived from the various websites using APIs and web-scraping technologies based on Python. I can't gaurantee the accuracy of financial data and other information you find in this platform. I don't recommend to buy or sell any of the stocks you may find in this website. I don't have any investment relationship with any of these companies. In the future, the financial data and information might be imported to this app from a reliable commercial sources if it is feasible. Until then, this is just an ongoing personal research project.

I would like to thank to all the providers of open source technologies, free APIs, tutorials, and all other tools which made this project possible. I hope this app becomes a useful source for the investors as it matures in future. Please visit the platform more frequently for the updates. Thank you for your visit.

Surya Gurung.
